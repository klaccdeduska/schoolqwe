Work name: "Reboot"

Year: 1994

Creator: Gavin Blair, John Grace, Philip Mitchell.

Genre: Adventure, Action

Runtime in minutes: 30

Episodes: 47

ReBoot was the story of Mainframe, a city in cyberspace, and the various "programs" (people) that lived within. Filled with its own mythology and unique phrases that references a lot of computer terminology, it was a very successful show wherever it was shown.

Work name: "Beast Wars"

Year: 1996

Creator: Steve Sacks, Colin Davies, John Pozer

Genre: Sci-Fi, Action

Runtime in minutes: 30

Episodes: 52

The Maximals were descendants of the Autobots, while the Predacons were descendants of the Decepticons. The series features the Maximals and Predacons battling to control an energy source that gives its owner supreme power over the universe.
